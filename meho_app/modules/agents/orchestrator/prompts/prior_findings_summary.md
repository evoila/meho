# Prior Findings Summary Format

When previous specialists have investigated, their findings are summarized as:

## Prior Investigation Findings

- **[Connector Name]** [OK]: Key finding in 1-2 sentences, truncated at ~300 chars.
- **[Connector Name]** [PARTIAL]: Partial finding with context.

Use these findings to guide your investigation. Do NOT re-investigate what was already found unless you have a specific reason.

---

This format is generated by context_passing.build_structured_prior_findings()
and injected via the {{prior_findings_context}} template variable.
