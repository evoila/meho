evoila is proud to open-source MEHO -- an AI-powered infrastructure investigation platform that connects to 15 systems and reasons across all of them in a single conversation.

MEHO was born from our own operational experience. Our managed services engineers work across Kubernetes, VMware, Prometheus, Loki, ArgoCD, and more every day. Cross-system incidents -- where the root cause spans multiple infrastructure layers -- are the hardest to diagnose and the most time-consuming. MEHO automates that investigation.

Today we're releasing the full platform under AGPLv3:

- All 15 connectors (Kubernetes, VMware, Proxmox, GCP, Prometheus, Loki, Tempo, Alertmanager, ArgoCD, GitHub, Jira, Confluence, REST, SOAP) -- open source, no restrictions
- Cross-system entity resolution and topology mapping
- Multi-LLM support including fully air-gapped deployments with Ollama
- Docker Compose quickstart -- running in 5 minutes

Community solves the technical problem. Enterprise solves the organizational problem.
