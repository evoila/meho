        Close button -- pure client-side reset of the drawer slot. We
        intentionally do NOT use HTMX here: ``return false;`` in an
        inline ``onclick`` does not cancel HTMX's separately-registered

        ``onclick`` fired two click handlers (a phantom GET to an
        undefined endpoint plus the local DOM swap). Dropping the
        ``hx-*`` attributes leaves the inline ``onclick`` as the sole
        handler -- no network round trip required for a Close.
